About Us

Welcome to Manzil Explorers, your trusted guide in navigating academic and career paths. We specialize in providing comprehensive career guidance to students in 10th grade, 12th grade, and post-graduation. Our Mission To empower students with the knowledge and confidence to make informed decisions about their futures. What We Do For 10th Graders: Personalized guidance to choose the right stream based on interests and strengths. For 12th Graders: Tailored advice on college applications, scholarships, and course selection. For Post-Graduation: Career counseling, job search strategies, resume building, and interview preparation.
